∮Lf⃗⋅dl⃗=∫S(∇×f⃗)⋅dS⃗\oint_L \vec{f}\cdot d\vec{l} = \int_S(\nabla \times \vec{f})\cdot d\vec{S} ∮L​f​⋅dl=∫S​(∇×f​)⋅dS

∮(ψ∇ϕ−ϕ∇ψ)⋅dS⃗=∫V(ψ∇2ϕ−ϕ∇2ψ)dV\oint(\psi\nabla\phi-\phi\nabla\psi)\cdot d\vec{S}=\int_V(\psi\nabla^2\phi-\phi\nabla^2\psi)dV ∮(ψ∇ϕ−ϕ∇ψ)⋅dS=∫V​(ψ∇2ϕ−ϕ∇2ψ)dV
